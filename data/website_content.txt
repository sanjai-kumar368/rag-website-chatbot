Network Frequency and Signal Quality

SkyNet Fiber operates on a wired fiber-optic network, which does not rely on radio frequencies for last-mile internet delivery. This eliminates common wireless issues such as signal interference, congestion, and weather-related disruptions.

For in-home connectivity, Wi-Fi routers provided by SkyNet Fiber operate on standard Wi-Fi frequency bands to distribute internet within the premises.

Wi-Fi Frequency Bands Explained

SkyNet Fiber routers support dual-band Wi-Fi technology.

2.4 GHz Frequency Band

Provides wider coverage area

Penetrates walls and obstacles more effectively

Suitable for browsing, emails, messaging, and smart devices

Recommended for larger homes where distance coverage is required

5 GHz Frequency Band

Provides higher internet speeds

Lower interference from neighboring networks

Ideal for online gaming, video conferencing, HD and 4K streaming

Best used when devices are closer to the router

Customers can choose the preferred band based on usage requirements.
